Lomi of Batangas

Lomi, also known as Pancit Lomi originated in Lipa Batangas, is a Filipino dish made using a variety of thick fresh egg noodles about a quarter of an inch in diameter that have been soaked in Iye water to provide texture

BukoPie & Pasalubong of Laguna

Buko pie is a Filipino-style coconut pie made of fresh, tender young coconut meat combined with a creamy filling and enclosed in a flaky pie crust. The pie is a favorite pasalubong item mostly bought from the area of Laguna where it’s known to have originated.

Bulalo of Cavite

Bulalo is simply a beef dish which is known to have originated here in the Philippines. It’s especially popular in Southern Luzon areas, such as Cavite. It is popularly known for having the beef shank and bone marrow, and letting its flavors melt into a super tasty broth.
