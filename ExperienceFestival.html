Itik Festival of Laguna

The Itik Festival is celebrated to honor the town's beginnings in Victoria Laguna. In November 2002, the first Itik Festival was held, attracting a large number of visitors and students from nearby towns who came to learn how itik is cultivated and products created from it. Street dancers from public and private schools are part of the Itik Festival's attractions.
